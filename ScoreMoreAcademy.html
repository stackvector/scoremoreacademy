Our Story
About ScoreMore Academy
Rooted in Noida, transforming futures across the Delhi-NCR region for over two decades.

Mrs. Sona Kumar

Founder & Lead Educator · 20+ Years

ScoreMore Academy, located in Royal Garden Estate, Sector 61, Noida, is an innovative and result-driven coaching centre renowned for its exceptional Maths and Science education. Led by the esteemed Mrs. Sona Kumar — a veteran educator with over two decades of hands-on classroom expertise — the academy stands as the benchmark of excellence in secondary education.

With a relentless focus on concept-building and an unwavering commitment to nurturing a solid academic foundation, ScoreMore Academy prioritizes deep subject understanding over surface-level exam tricks. Through meticulously crafted assignments and worksheets, students benefit from rigorous practice while receiving personalized attention in deliberately small batch sizes.
