About ScoreMore Academy

Two decades of transforming how students relate to Maths and Science in Noida.

Founder & Lead Educator · 20+ Years

Mrs. Sona Kumar

ScoreMore Academy, located in Royal Garden Estate, Sector 61, Noida, is an innovative and result-driven coaching centre renowned for exceptional Maths and Science education.

Led by Mrs. Sona Kumar — a veteran educator with over two decades of hands-on classroom experience — the academy has built a reputation as one of Noida's most trusted names in secondary coaching. Her approach is built on genuine care, deep subject knowledge, and an ability to make even the most challenging concepts feel accessible.

With a relentless focus on concept-building and an unwavering commitment to nurturing solid academic foundations, ScoreMore Academy prioritises deep subject understanding over surface-level exam tricks.

  • ✓ CBSE, ICSE, IB and IGCSE board specialisation
  • ✓ Custom worksheets and assignment-based deep practice
  • ✓ Flexible offline and online class options
